A word on development

Best practices, latest trends, and the importance of team knowledge

Our development culture is very collaborative and open. As a member of the development team you're encouraged to share insights and ideas, latest coding techniques and best practices, architecture critiques, or even just discussing the latest platform APIs or trends to be continually raising the bar of what is technically possible.

Knowledge can be gained by curiosity – don’t be afraid to ask when you don’t know something. There’ll be areas where your knowledge is deeper than others, and together we can become a stronger team by encouraging open and honest discussion, and striving to be at the forefront of technology.

Core Tools

Eclipse, Android Studio, Git clients

We currently use Eclipse for Android development, but will soon be moving to Android Studio. The choice of Git client is up to you, but within the team SmartGit and GitBox are commonly used. If there are any tools you’ve used which you think would be useful to share with the team, we encourage you to share any learnings you have.

Keeping up-to-date

Online resources, meetups, events

Staying in touch with industry news, events, tools and emerging trends is really important to us. We encourage you to attend meetups and events, keep up to date with the online research, and share any knowledge with the team by means of lunch and learns, development team sessions or by contributing to our company wide development wiki.
