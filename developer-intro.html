Your role

The (multi disciplined) designer

A word on design

Best practice and industry standard

We are proactive in the application of best practice and industry standard design. The "PPP3" (Pixel Perfect Precision 3) Guide to digital design best reflects this approach. You can find the guide on the design server under 'resources'.

Our design culture is also very collaborative and open. As a member of the design team you're encouraged to share insights and ideas, get involved in design critiques, conduct 'lunch & learn' sessions and promote design thinking to the broader company.

Core Tools

Adobe + others = good design

We use Adobe CC for the design process, along with a number of other third party tools that help us improve efficiencies in workflow and collaboration.

Keeping up-to-date

Tigerspike experience blog

Staying in touch with industry new, events, tools and emerging trends is really important to us. Feel free to share insights on our global Experience Design Blog
